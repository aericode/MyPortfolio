About Me

Hello, my name is Eric. I’m a developer focused in front-end web programming since 2019.


My interest in programming began with game development. As a kid, I was always curious about how videogames were made. As a teenager, in 2008 I started to try and learn how to make my own games.


Programming started as a hobby, and later became my profession. I got a bachelor degree in I.T. at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte in 2020 and found in making websites and sharing my knowledge new amazing ways of creating experiences that people could enjoy.

  • React
